## 1. Introduction

Welcome to SharaSpot! These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern your use of the SharaSpot mobile application and services ("Service"). By using our Service, you agree to these Terms.

### 1.1 About SharaSpot

SharaSpot operates as a marketplace platform connecting electric vehicle (EV) users with charging station owners. We facilitate charging transactions and payments but do not own or operate charging stations.

### 1.2 GST Status

**Important Notice**: SharaSpot is currently **not registered under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act, 2017**. Our annual turnover is below the ₹40 lakhs registration threshold. This means:

- We cannot issue GST invoices
- We cannot charge GST separately
- All prices are inclusive and final
- We provide payment receipts (not tax invoices)

This is fully compliant with Indian tax laws for businesses operating below the GST threshold.

## 2. Pricing and Payments

### 2.1 Transparent Pricing Model

All prices displayed on SharaSpot are inclusive and transparent. There are no hidden fees.

**Price Components**:
1. **Charging Cost**: Energy consumed (kWh) × Rate per kWh
2. **Platform Fee**: SharaSpot commission (currently 5% of charging cost)
3. **Total Amount**: Sum of above (final amount you pay)

### 2.2 No GST Charges

As we are not GST registered:
- GST is NOT charged separately
- Prices do NOT include separate GST line items
- All amounts are final and inclusive

### 2.3 Future GST Registration

If SharaSpot's turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs, we will:
- Register under GST as required by law
- Notify users 30 days in advance
- Update pricing to include GST breakdown
- Begin issuing tax invoices
- Maintain full transparency throughout the transition

### 2.4 Payment Receipts

After each transaction, you will receive:
- Digital payment receipt (not a tax invoice)
- Email confirmation
- In-app transaction history
- PDF download option

All receipts clearly state: **"This is a payment receipt and not a tax invoice. SharaSpot is not registered under GST."**

### 2.5 Payment Processing

- Payments processed via Razorpay (RBI-authorized payment gateway)
- Supported methods: UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, Wallets
- All payments in Indian Rupees (INR)
- Transactions are encrypted and secure

### 2.6 Refund Policy

- Charging transactions are **non-refundable** once completed
- Failed payments are automatically refunded within 5-7 business days
- Disputed charges reviewed on case-by-case basis
- Contact support@sharaspot.com for refund requests
